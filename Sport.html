Sports

I have a keen interest in sporting activity. From a young age I have played many different sports. These include:

  • Hurling - Butlerstown GAA
  • Soccer - Villa F.C.
  • Ultimate Frisbee - UCC Ultimate
  • Rugby - Waterford City RFC

Hurling

Without a doubt, hurling was my first love in the sporting world.
Whether it be playing it myself amongst my school friends or cheering on my county team in Croke Park (not that they got there often...),
I was a devote follower of the game.


Soccer

Always an avid fan of Arsenal F.C., my love of the game has sadly been reducing with each passing season.
A member of Villa F.C. since infancy, never my best sport.


Rugby

Late starter to rugby. First played in school and then with Waterford City Rugby Club.
It is my favourite sport out of the lot and would never trade the experiences I had with it for anything else in the world.


Ultimate

The sport I picked up in college.
I find it a very beneficial way to both have fun and keep fit.
